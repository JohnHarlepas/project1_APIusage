FAQ'S

Are you licensed and insured?
Yes we are fully Licensed and Insured, for any Liabilities.
Are you a big national chain?
No. We have kept to our original company mission statement over twelve years ago to remain a small reputable business. This has allowed us to stay completely focused on providing the absolute highest quality of service possible.
What are your prices?
Our prices vary based on different packages and specific vehicle. We work to keep our prices up to date and inline with industry standards. Providing general website pricing can be misleading. We provide upfront individual pricing for your specific vehicle. This allows us to plan appropriate time and results and provide a very high level of customer satisfaction.
What is the best way to schedule?
Click Here!
What do you need?
For over 12 years we have operated daily with all necessary professional equipment with us. Our fully functional mobile units carry our own water tank and electric power source. Our units are completely capable of properly hand washing your vehicle, steam cleaning/extracting and sanitizing interior upholstery, and we have invested in modern polishers, tools and use a variety of current high quality detailing products allowing us to provide the best detailing possible. Our units are only operated by highly skilled professional detailers with a minimum of 10 years employment with our company.
Do I need to provide water or electricity?
Our detailers arrive at your location fully-equipped with all the supplies they need to ensure that your car is spotless, including a generator for power supply. If you prefer a water-based wash, we do ask that you have a water supply at the location. We also offer waterless (eco-friendly) washes which produce the same result and are environmentally friendly. If you don’t specifically request a water-based wash, we will assign the first detailer available in your area.
What is a waterless carwash?
A waterless car wash is a car wash that is done using a high quality, concentrated solution that have been engineered to produce the same washing results as a water-based car wash. By using this method we actually save tons of water and is our way to help preserve the environment. It also allows a detailers to perform services in tight areas.
Can you service the vehicle in any place?
Even though the detailers on our platform are miracle workers, they won’t be able to teleport their car inside your office parking and detail your car between two other cars! We wish they could but we are still working on that. Hence, you need to have your car easily accessible for our detailers when they arrive. How do I schedule an appointment?
It's easy! Simply navigate to the contact page,here. Once you enter your information, your booking is completed you will receive a confirmation email as well as a text notification. If you need help placing a booking or have - questions about the packages please reach out to us directly at (855) 210-1337.
How do I pay for the service?
We are a cashless service so you will need an active debit/credit card. All payments are made on the website through a secure and easy to use platform.
How will you access the car?
The detailer will call you upon arriving at the location. You can let the detailer know where the car is located or show him/her to the vehicle. You also have an option of leaving instructions when placing the booking.
What if I have more than one car?
No problem. Just type in the additional vehicle when making an appointment and we shall provide you with an accurate estimate accordingly.
